Venue

The tournament is held at River Pool & Sports Bar, located at 19 NW 5th Ave, Downtown Portland.
The venue is easily accessible by public transit and offers a welcoming atmosphere for players and spectators alike. Pool is always FREE at River — first come, first served for practice before the tournament.

Transportation

MAX Light Rail

Two MAX Light Rail stations are located within a short walk of the venue:

  • NW 5th & Couch: Served by the Green, Orange, and Yellow MAX lines, as well as the Blue and Yellow bus lines.
  • NW 6th & Davis: Also served by the Green, Orange, and Yellow MAX lines, plus Blue and Yellow bus lines.

Parking

  • On-street metered parking: Available throughout downtown Portland. Always check signage for time limits and payment details.
  • SmartPark Garages: Five public garages are operated by the city nearby. Visit the Portland Parking Guide for locations and rates.
  • Private lots: Additional paid lots and garages are within walking distance. Availability and prices may vary.
