📍 Venue 📍

River Pool & Sports Bar: 19 NW 5th Ave, Downtown Portland

Easily accessible by public transit, River offers a welcoming atmosphere for players and spectators alike.

Free Pool 24/7/365: First come, first served — warm up, share the table, and don’t be a ball hog. 😉

🚗 Parking 🚗

  • On-street metered parking: Available throughout downtown. Check signage for time limits and payment details.
  • SmartPark Garages: Several city-run garages nearby. See the Portland Parking Guide.
  • Private lots: Paid lots and garages also available. Rates and availability vary.

🔒 As with any downtown venue, we kindly remind players to lock their vehicles and avoid leaving valuables inside.🔒
