Frequently Asked Questions

Who can play in these tournaments?

Any player with a Fargo rating of 445 and below is welcome! You'll need to show your Fargo account and a photo ID at registration.

Do I need to register ahead of time?

It’s recommended! You can submit your Fargo screenshot in the Sign Up sheet, but your rating will be confirmed live in the Fargo app at registration.

How much is the entry fee?

As of June 3, 2025, the entry fee is $15 (cash only) and must be paid at the time of registration. $10 goes directly into the pot, while $5 is an admin fee. Fees can change at the tournament director’s discretion, and any house adds will be announced at registration.

What is the admin fee?

The $5 admin fee covers the tournament director’s time, website maintenance and updates, Fargo fees to report tournament results, and other overhead costs needed to keep the tournament running smoothly.

What’s the race format?

Race lengths vary by the number of players and Fargo gaps:

  • 8–12 players: race to 3 wins
  • 13–16 players: race to 2 wins
  • If there’s a 75+ Fargo gap, the higher-rated player must win 1 extra game

Are there any rules I should know about?

We follow modified BCA rules, with adjustments made by the tournament director as needed. No jump shots are allowed, and players must mark their 8-ball pocket during 8-ball games.

Do I have to stay the whole night?

Matches run one after the other, so you’re welcome to watch, play, or hang out at the bar in between. But once your bracket’s done, you’re good to go!

Can I bring in outside food or drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but River Pool & Sports Bar has plenty of delicious food and beverage options. Don’t forget to tip your bartenders!

Where is the tournament held?

We play at River Pool & Sports Bar, 19 NW 5th Ave, Downtown Portland. It’s easily accessible via MAX, and off-street paid parking is available.

Can I use my own rack?

Magic Racks are fine if both players agree. If not, triangle racks are the default.

Is there a house add to the pot?

House adds are not guaranteed, but if they’re offered for the night, they’ll be announced at registration.

Where can I park near River Pool & Sports Bar?

There are several parking options near River Pool & Sports Bar (19 NW 5th Ave, Portland, OR 97209):

  • On-street metered parking: Available throughout downtown Portland. Be sure to check signage for time limits and payment requirements.
  • SmartPark Garages: The city operates five public garages nearby. More information can be found on the Portland Parking Guide.
  • Private parking lots: Numerous private lots and garages are in the vicinity. It's advisable to check availability and rates in advance.

What is the nearest MAX Light Rail station?

The closest MAX Light Rail stations to River Pool & Sports Bar (19 NW 5th Ave) are:

  • NW 5th & Couch: Served by the Green, Orange, and Yellow MAX lines; also Blue and Yellow bus lines.
  • NW 6th & Davis: Served by the Green, Orange, and Yellow MAX lines; also Blue and Yellow bus lines.
