Tournament Rules

Player Eligibility & Registration

  • Fargo ratings 445 and below accepted for 8, 9, and 10-ball tournaments.
  • All players must present a Fargo account and valid photo ID at registration.
  • First-time players must create a Fargo account at registration.
  • If multiple Fargo accounts exist for a player, the highest rated account will be used. Players over 445 will be disqualified. Submit a request to Fargo to merge accounts if needed.
  • Submit a screenshot of your Fargo rating to reserve your spot in the tournament.
  • Ranking at the time of registration on tournament night determines eligibility and race lengths.

Match Format & Races

  • 8–12 players: race to 3 wins.
  • 13–16 players: race to 2 wins.
  • 75+ Fargo rate gap: higher-rated player must win 1 additional game.
  • Games follow BCA rules with modifications as needed by the tournament director.

Equipment & Gameplay

  • Racks will be Fargo Reported.
  • Players lag or flip a coin for the break, then alternate breaks. If no agreement, lag by default.
  • Magic Racks approved if available. Players must agree on rack style before the match. Triangle used by default if no agreement.
  • Jump shots are not allowed at River.
  • No timeouts. Rule questions welcomed—ask the tournament director if unsure.
  • If you need a shot watched, ask the tournament director.

Conduct & House Rules

  • No drinks on or near pool tables. Spills or damage result in permanent disqualification.
  • Tournament director reserves the right to adjust rules as needed.
  • House does not promise to always donate to the pot.
  • Entry fees are cash only and may change.
